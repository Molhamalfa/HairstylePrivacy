3. Face Data Policy

StyleSwap utilizes advanced AI technology to provide hairstyle and color simulations. We take your privacy regarding biological data extremely seriously.

1) What face data does your app collect?

Face data is not collected by the app. Face data is processed only in real time and never stored or retained after analysis.

2) How do you use the collected face data?

Face data is processed only in real time for feature delivery, never stored or shared. The app analyzes the face and the position of lips, nose and eyes in the photo to apply the virtual hairstyles and colors accurately. The app deletes all temporary data immediately after the analysis is complete.

3) Will the data be shared with any third parties? Where will this information be stored?

Data will not be shared with any third parties. The app will not store any face data, original images, or biological information on our servers.

4) How long will face data be retained?

No face data is collected or saved by the app. All processing occurs in a transient state, and data is wiped immediately after the analysis is complete.

5) Where in your privacy policy do you explain this?

This "Face Data Policy" (Section 3) explicitly details our collection, use, disclosure, sharing, and retention practices.
